# Analysis

## Layer 8, Head 11

This head pays attention to the direct objects of verbs. It gives more attention to the substantive.

Example Sentences:

- She checks the [MASK] before bathing.
- She plays the piano every [MASK].
- James and Mike play the piano every [MASK].

## Layer 2, Head 5

This head pays attention to the word immediately before the current, opposing Layer 3, Head 10

Example Sentences:

- John likes [MASK] well done.
- I play [MASK] with friends every day.
